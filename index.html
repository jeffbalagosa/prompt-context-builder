Template guide

  1. # Role

    Define the persona, expertise, or responsibilities the assistant must embrace.

  2. # Objective

    State the high-level goal or outcome the assistant should achieve.

  3. # Instructions

    List specific guidance, rules, or nuances for the assistant to follow.

  4. # Context

    Provide background such as documents, users, or environmental details.

  5. # Example(s)

    Share good vs. bad examples to shape the assistant's outputs.

  6. # Constraints

    Clarify guardrails like time, scope, or compliance needs.

  7. # Output Style

    Describe tone or presentation expectations.

  8. # Output Format

    Specify structural requirements (tables, bullet lists, etc.).

  9. # Output Verbosity

    Choose the desired detail level: concise, medium, high, or ultra.

  10. # Thinking Effort

    Pick how much reasoning to apply: quick, medium, high, or ultra.
